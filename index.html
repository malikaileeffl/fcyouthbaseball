Our Mission

Baseball for Every Kid

There are kids right here in southern Indiana who want to play, who have the drive and the passion — but their families just can't make it happen financially. Registration fees, equipment, uniforms… it adds up fast.

FC Youth Baseball was built on one simple belief: no child should sit on the sidelines because money is tight.

We provide completely free participation — no registration fees, no equipment costs, no hidden charges. Just baseball, teamwork, and a community that shows up for its kids.

About Hayden

The Man Behind the Mission

My name is Hayden Keltner, and I'm from right here in southern Indiana — born and raised. Baseball has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember, and this game taught me things that still carry me today.

How to fail and get back up. How to be part of something bigger than yourself. How to work hard for something you love.

When I started seeing kids in our community missing out on all of that — not because they didn't want it, but because their families were struggling — I knew I had to do something.

FC Youth Baseball is my answer to that. This league exists because every kid in southern Indiana deserves a chance to feel what it's like to be part of a team.
